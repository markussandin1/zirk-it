# Hero Section Generation

Create a compelling hero section that immediately captures visitor attention and communicates the business value proposition.

## Input Variables:
- `{businessName}`: The business name
- `{industry}`: The business industry
- `{description}`: Brief business description
- `{location}`: Business location (if provided)

## Requirements:

### Title (Main Headline):
- Maximum 8 words
- Include business name OR core value proposition
- Must be compelling and memorable
- Industry-appropriate language
- Should create immediate interest

### Subtitle (Supporting Text):
- 10-20 words
- Expand on the title with specific benefits
- Address the primary customer need
- Include location if relevant for local businesses
- Should build trust and credibility

### CTA Text (Call-to-Action):
- 2-4 words maximum
- Action-oriented verbs
- Industry-appropriate (e.g., "Book Now", "Get Quote", "Shop Now", "Call Today")
- Create urgency or desire
- Should be the most logical next step for visitors

## Industry Guidelines:

**Restaurant/Food:**
- Focus on experience, taste, atmosphere
- Use sensory language
- CTAs: "Reserve Table", "Order Now", "View Menu"

**Professional Services:**
- Emphasize expertise and results
- Build trust and authority
- CTAs: "Get Consultation", "Schedule Call", "Request Quote"

**Retail/E-commerce:**
- Highlight products and value
- Focus on selection and quality
- CTAs: "Shop Now", "Browse Collection", "Find Deals"

**Health/Wellness:**
- Emphasize care and outcomes
- Use reassuring language
- CTAs: "Book Appointment", "Learn More", "Schedule Consultation"

## Output Format:
```json
{
  "title": "Your compelling main headline",
  "subtitle": "Supporting text that builds on the title and creates interest",
  "cta_text": "Action Button"
}
```

Remember: The hero section is the first impression - make it count!
