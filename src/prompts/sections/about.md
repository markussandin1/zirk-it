# About Section Generation

Create a compelling about section that builds trust, establishes credibility, and connects emotionally with potential customers.

## Input Variables:
- `{businessName}`: The business name
- `{industry}`: The business industry  
- `{description}`: User-provided business description
- `{services}`: List of services/products offered

## Requirements:

### Title:
- 2-4 words
- Professional and appropriate for industry
- Options: "About Us", "Our Story", "Who We Are", "Our Mission"
- Should feel personal yet professional

### Description:
- 80-150 words
- Transform user's basic description into professional copy
- Include 3 key elements:
  1. **What you do** (expand on services with benefits)
  2. **Why you're different** (unique value proposition)
  3. **Who you serve** (target customer connection)

## Content Elements to Include:
- **Expertise/Experience**: Establish credibility
- **Customer Focus**: Show you understand their needs  
- **Results/Benefits**: What customers can expect
- **Personal Touch**: Add warmth and relatability
- **Trust Signals**: Quality, reliability, professionalism

## Industry-Specific Approaches:

**Restaurant:**
- Story of passion for food
- Quality ingredients and preparation
- Creating memorable experiences
- Family/community connection

**Professional Services:**
- Years of experience and expertise
- Client success stories (general)
- Industry knowledge and insights
- Commitment to results

**Retail:**
- Curated selection process
- Quality and value focus
- Customer service excellence
- Understanding customer needs

**Health/Wellness:**
- Caring approach to health
- Professional qualifications
- Patient-centered care
- Commitment to outcomes

## Tone Guidelines:
- **Confident but not boastful**
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Specific but not overly technical**
- **Warm but not overly casual**

## Output Format:
```json
{
  "title": "About Section Title",
  "description": "A compelling 80-150 word description that builds trust, establishes credibility, and connects with customers by explaining what you do, why you're different, and who you serve."
}
```

Remember: This section should make visitors think "These are the people I want to work with!"
