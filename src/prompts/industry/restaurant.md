# Restaurant Industry Prompts

## Industry Context:
Restaurants succeed by creating memorable dining experiences that combine great food, atmosphere, and service. Focus on sensory language, emotional connection, and the complete dining experience.

## Key Customer Motivators:
- Quality and taste of food
- Dining atmosphere and ambiance  
- Service quality and hospitality
- Value for money
- Special occasions and experiences
- Convenience (location, hours, reservations)

## Language Style:
- **Warm and inviting** tone
- **Sensory-rich** descriptions (taste, aroma, texture, visual)
- **Experiential** language (memorable, special, authentic)
- **Community-focused** (family, friends, gathering)

## Common Services to Enhance:
- "Pizza" → "Wood-fired pizzas with authentic Italian flavors"
- "Catering" → "Full-service catering that makes your event unforgettable"
- "Breakfast" → "Fresh breakfast classics made with local ingredients"
- "Delivery" → "Hot, fresh meals delivered straight to your door"
- "Private Events" → "Intimate private dining experiences for your special occasions"

## Hero Section Focus:
- Emphasize the dining experience
- Use location if neighborhood-focused
- Include signature dishes or cuisine type
- Create appetite appeal

## About Section Focus:
- Story behind the restaurant (family recipes, chef background, local ingredients)
- Commitment to quality and freshness
- Atmosphere and dining experience
- Community connection

## Services Section Focus:
- Describe food with sensory language
- Mention preparation methods (wood-fired, hand-made, fresh daily)
- Include dietary options (vegetarian, gluten-free)
- Highlight signature or popular items

## Contact Section Focus:
- Reservation information prominent
- Hours of operation clearly stated
- Location and parking details
- Special event booking information

## Common CTAs:
- "Reserve Your Table"  
- "Order for Delivery"
- "View Our Menu"
- "Book Private Event"
- "Call for Reservations"

## SEO Keywords to Integrate:
- [Location] restaurant
- [Cuisine type] near me
- Best [food type] in [location]
- Restaurant reservations
- Private dining
- Catering services
